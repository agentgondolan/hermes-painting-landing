# Stand Canonical Spec — 2026-03-24

A simple wooden canvas stand made from 4 straight wooden slats. The stand is minimal, practical, and lightweight, with a natural handmade look. Real-life height is approximately 70 cm.

## Structure
The stand consists of exactly 4 wooden parts:
1. front left leg
2. front right leg
3. rear support leg
4. one horizontal bottom brace connecting the two front legs

## Overall form
The two front legs stand on the ground and rise upward in a symmetric inverted V shape. They meet together at the top in one shared joint. A third rear leg is attached to the same top joint and extends backward to the floor, creating a stable triangular support. Near the bottom, a horizontal wooden brace connects the two front legs.

## Side view

## Bottom brace
